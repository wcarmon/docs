# Setup
```sh
go install github.com/google/wire/cmd/wire@latest;
```
1. Ensure `$GOPATH/bin` is on your `$PATH`
    1. eg. `$HOME/go/bin/wire`
1. Alternative: symlink to ~/bin
    1. `ln -vs $HOME/go/bin/wire $HOME/bin`
